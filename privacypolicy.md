# Privacy Policy - You Shall Not Pass Extension

**Effective Date:** Friday, November 21st, 2025

## What We Collect
This extension collects:
- URLs visited (to detect proxy/gaming sites)
- Tab creation patterns (to enforce limits)
- Violation events (iframe floods, history manipulation)

## How We Use Data
- All data is stored locally on the student's Chromebook
- Data is used only for security monitoring by school IT staff
- Logs auto-delete after 30 days
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No personal information is collected

## Data Access
- Only school IT administrators can access logs
- Students cannot view or modify logs
- No third parties have access to this data
